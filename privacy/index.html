1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal information you disclose to us

In Short: We do not collect or process any personal information.

The MeditateEEG app operates entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All meditation sessions, preferences, and data remain stored locally on your device only.

Sensitive Information. We do not process sensitive information.

Application Data. The app may request permission for:

  • Push Notifications. We may request to send you push notifications regarding meditation reminders or app features. If you wish to opt out from receiving these types of communications, you may turn them off in your device's settings.

Meditation Session Storage. After each session, the app lets you decide whether to:

  • save the full session data (waveforms, timestamps, and calculated metrics);
  • save a limited summary (duration and average meditation level only); or
  • discard the session entirely.

All session data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us or any third-party server. Note: Session data storage is only available with an active paid subscription. In the free limited mode, sessions are not saved.

No personal data, analytics, crash logs, or device identifiers are collected or transmitted to external servers.

15. PAYMENTS AND SUBSCRIPTIONS

The app offers paid features via a monthly subscription. All transactions are securely processed through Google Play Billing. The app does not collect, store, or process any credit card or financial data. All payment-related data is managed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

Free Trial and Limited Mode. The app starts in a limited-feature mode that shows only the home screen and basic meditation playback. When you activate the 14-day free trial, all premium features become available. If the subscription is not purchased within 24 hours after the trial ends, the app automatically returns to limited-feature mode (no session saving or analytics) until a subscription is activated.

The standard monthly price is displayed in your local currency on Google Play before you confirm the subscription.

16. SUBSCRIPTION MANAGEMENT

Users can manage or cancel their subscriptions at any time via their Google Play account settings. Details about the subscription terms, renewal policy, and cancellation procedure are available within the app and on the Google Play store listing.

After the trial expires (or if a subscription is canceled), the app reverts to a limited-feature mode that allows basic meditation tracking but with restricted analytics and history. Users can resubscribe at any time to regain full functionality.
